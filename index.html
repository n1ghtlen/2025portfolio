Fun Questionnaire

I created this quiz using HTML/CSS and vanilla JavaScript that collects the user's contact information along with their answers to see if they answered the same as I would. Upon completing the survey, their contact information is displayed along with their result.

If our answers aren't quite the same, the user receives the message displayed in the video.

If we answer exactly the same, the message would read "We're pretty alike you and me!"

Portfolio Design

This is the general design outline I created in Figma for the portfolio you're looking at right now, crafted to reflect my personality through a mix of soft pastels.

I love incorporating these tones as they allow me to explore color in a way that keeps the design minimalistic, sleek, and easy on the eyes.

Accessibility is a priority in my design choices, and I find that subtle palettes allow me to explore color while keeping accessibility in mind.

Taking a design from visual conception to code is one of the most exciting parts of web development for me and is a major reason I’m drawn to this field!

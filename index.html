Local Florist Landing Page

This is the landing page for Prairie Rose, my version of a website design for a florist. I developed the logo concept using Illustrator and created the layout in Figma.

I was inspired to create this concept after seeing the websites of some of my local florists, as many have jarring color schemes and outdated designs.

Keeping in mind current design trends, I chose MegaFresh for my logo concept, and Switzer Regular, Semibold and Extrabold for headers and paragraph text.

I also opted for light pinks with simple black and white UI to compliment and soften the dark pink present in the design.

"Oneiria" App Concept MidFi

This is one of the first mid-fidelty prototypes I created using Figma of a proposed app I came up with called Oneiria, a social platform where users can document and discuss or analyze their dreams in a social media format with other users. The name "Oneiria" is inspired by the Greek word "oneiros" which is the personification of dreams in Greek mythology.

I didn’t necessarily find any particular platforms as direct inspiration for this, but I did look at other forum platforms like Reddit and Quora to get an idea of how the site should generally be structured.

I like the idea of having communities, especially when users have certain reasons for being on the site and can interact with other users who express themselves in a similar format. This is sort of a comparable concept to subreddits on Reddit. I also wanted to give users topics that they could explore when they’re not sure what to look for, similar to other forum websites.

I wanted to keep it simple enough but also add the color and whimsical design that other platforms don’t have due to the nature of the website.

"ShelfMate" App Concept HiFi

This is a hifi app concept created in Figma for ShelfMate, a tool in development with the goal of allowing users to track their grocery purchases and make decisions based on their dietary preferences or restrictions. The app allows the user to:

Use the barcode scanning function and alternatively a receipt scanning function (using OCR technology) to upload items to their virtual pantry

View information of a product in order to make informed decisions

See recipes using the items in the user's pantry

View a personal pantry curated by the user with quantity and nutritional information (in progress)
